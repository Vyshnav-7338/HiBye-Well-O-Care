About Us

HiBye Well”O”Care is a dedicated platform that fosters social cohesion, community building, and emotional wellbeing through the power of art, culture, and entertainment. We believe in the transformative impact of creative expression and strive to create a vibrant and inclusive community for all.

About Us
Our Vision

To be a leading force in fostering artistic expression, enhancing social wellbeing, and uniting communities, creating a vibrant and inclusive space where everyone feels valued and connected.

Our Mission
  • Empowering Creativity: We provide a stage for diverse artistic expressions, offering a wide range of programs, workshops, and events that cater to various age groups and interests.
  • Building Community: We foster a sense of belonging and connection by creating safe, inclusive, and welcoming spaces for relaxation, stress reduction, and overall mental health.
  • Supporting Local Talent: We champion local artists, performers, and creatives by providing opportunities for professional development, community engagement, and collaboration.
  • Promoting Social Inclusion: We ensure access to entertainment and leisure activities for underserved and marginalized communities, working with other organizations to amplify our impact.
  • Prioritizing Mental Health: We offer holistic support through initiatives like music therapy sessions, counseling services, and mental health awareness programs.
Our Objectives
  • To provide a platform for artistic expression and entertainment that promotes social cohesion and community building.
  • To create a safe and inclusive space for people to relax, unwind, and escape the stresses of everyday life.
  • To offer a diverse range of entertainment programs, workshops, and events that cater to different age groups, interests, and abilities.
  • To foster a sense of belonging and connection among members and participants, promoting emotional well-being and social support.
  • To provide access to entertainment and leisure activities for underserved or marginalized communities, promoting social inclusion and equity.
  • To support local artists, performers, and creatives, providing opportunities for professional development and community engagement.
  • To create a comfortable and welcoming environment that promotes relaxation, reduces stress, and improves overall mental health and well-being.
  • To build partnerships and collaborations with other organizations, businesses, and stakeholders to amplify impact and resources.
  • To provide mental support structures and resources for individuals struggling with mental health issues, such as music therapy sessions, access to counseling services, and support groups for mental health awareness and advocacy.

Join Us

Whether you're an aspiring artist, a passionate enthusiast, or simply seeking a creative outlet, we invite you to become a part of our vibrant community. Together, let's celebrate the power of creativity and make a positive impact on our world.

HIBYE Entertainment: Connecting People, Creating Joy

In partnership with HiBye Entertainment, we believe that happiness and connection are vital to building a thriving community. We are excited to present HIBYE Entertainment—a collection of innovative mobile applications designed to unite people based on shared interests, vibes, and mindsets.

Our Apps: HIBYE MID & HIBYE ELD

HIBYE MID and HIBYE ELD are crafted to help users discover like-minded individuals, fostering genuine relationships and joyful experiences. Whether bonding over common hobbies, exploring new interests, or simply enjoying quality time with others, our apps make connecting effortless and enjoyable.

  • HIBYE MID: Tailored for users looking to connect with friends within their age group, featuring tools that enable sharing, exploration, and engagement with those who share their passions and energy.
  • HIBYE ELD: Designed for individuals wishing to connect with others at a similar life stage, offering opportunities for meaningful conversations, shared activities, and a vibrant social life.

At HIBYE Entertainment, our goal is to create inclusive spaces where everyone feels welcome and understood, making it easy to find happiness and companionship through shared moments.

HIBYE EVOs: Innovating Social Connections

We are committed to leveraging the latest technologies to enhance community, connection, and understanding. In addition to our social connectivity apps, HIBYE MID and HIBYE ELD, we are excited to introduce our latest venture into immersive technology with HIBYE VR and AR, in collaboration with Endurevo.

HIBYE VR and AR: Bringing Immersive Technology to All

With our HIBYE VR and AR project, we aim to make cutting-edge virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR) experiences accessible to everyone. Partnering with Endurevo, we’re creating platforms and applications that allow users to explore VR and AR environments, gain hands-on experience with AI, and understand the potential of these transformative technologies.

  • Inclusive Access: Ensuring that VR and AR are not limited to tech experts but are available to all community members, regardless of their experience level.
  • Educational Programs: Offering workshops and interactive sessions where users can learn about AI, VR, and AR in an engaging, hands-on way.
  • Enhanced Experiences: Providing a platform where users can explore virtual worlds, participate in AI-driven simulations, and connect with others in innovative ways.

Through this project, we aim to bridge the gap between people and technology, offering a unique opportunity for everyone to experience, understand, and benefit from the advancements in AI, VR, and AR.

HIBYE Community: Building Lasting Connections

At HIBYE, we understand that true connection goes beyond just technology. We believe in fostering a community where individuals can share experiences, insights, and support each other on their journeys. Our community initiatives are designed to create a welcoming environment for everyone.

Community Initiatives: Connecting Through Shared Experiences

Our community initiatives focus on bringing people together through various activities, events, and online platforms. We aim to create spaces where users can engage meaningfully and develop relationships that last.

  • Events and Workshops: Organizing regular events and workshops that encourage participants to learn, collaborate, and connect over common interests.
  • Online Forums: Establishing online forums and discussion groups where users can share ideas, ask questions, and support each other.
  • Volunteer Opportunities: Creating volunteer programs that allow community members to give back, fostering a sense of belonging and shared purpose.

Through these initiatives, we strive to cultivate a vibrant community where everyone feels valued and empowered to connect, share, and grow together.

HIBYE Well-O-City: Nature for All

With a vision of inclusivity and well-being, HIBYE Well-O-City is designed to make the beauty of nature accessible to everyone. This initiative ensures that individuals from all financial backgrounds can experience the tranquility of natural spaces, connect with nature, and enjoy the restorative benefits of being outdoors.

Project Highlights:

  • Access to Nature Parks: Well-O-City offers entry to beautifully maintained parks and natural spaces, providing a place for people of all ages to explore, relax, and connect with the environment.
  • Inclusive Experiences: Regardless of financial background, everyone is welcome to immerse themselves in nature, learn about local ecology, and feel the rejuvenating energy of the outdoors.
  • Promoting Eco-Awareness: Through interactive sessions, eco-tours, and community gardening, Well-O-City fosters a sense of environmental responsibility and an appreciation for natural resources.

At HIBYE Well-O-City, we believe that everyone should have the chance to connect with nature, feel the earth beneath their feet, and experience the serenity it brings. This project is about making natural spaces and well-being accessible to all, creating a healthier, more connected community.

HIBYE Special Moments: Celebrating Together, Affordably

In keeping with our mission to bring people closer, HIBYE Special Moments is dedicated to helping individuals and families celebrate their loved ones' special days and events without financial stress. This initiative focuses on creating joyful, memorable celebrations that are both bright and budget-friendly.

Project Highlights:

  • Affordable Event Planning: Offering low-cost or free options for event essentials, from venue arrangements to decorations, making it easier to celebrate birthdays, anniversaries, and other milestones.
  • Community Spaces for Celebrations: Access to beautiful community spaces and parks, providing an inviting, natural setting for gatherings without the high costs.
  • Special Day Packages: Creative packages that include low-cost activities, group experiences, and personalized touches to help people celebrate meaningfully without breaking the bank.
  • DIY Workshops: Guiding participants in creating their own decorations, gifts, and more, adding a personal touch to special events while keeping costs low.

With HIBYE Special Moments, we believe that everyone deserves a vibrant, joyful celebration with loved ones, regardless of budget. By making these events accessible, we bring people together to create unforgettable memories that don’t come at a high price.
